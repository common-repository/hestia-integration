Integrate your store with Hestia Delivery and automatize your delivery sending information to the carrier.

== Description ==

This plugin is used to integrate your store with Hestia Delivery, sending information to prepare your order when everything is ready. It makes your work easier.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `hestia-integration` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Config the plugin in Hestia Settings
